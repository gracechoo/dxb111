Victoria Park

Victoria Park is located in Herston, approximately 10 minutes away from Brisbane CBD. The park currently has an 18-hole golf course spanning 45 hectares but is soon to be developed into a Public Parkland as announced by Adrian Schrinner, the Lord Mayor of Brisbane. According to Schrinner, the re-development has the intention of "bringing nature back into the city" and "having something for everybody" includes a Cultural hub, Community vegetable patch, a lake, canopy walk and more!

Transport by car/taxi

Victoria Park is less than 10 minutes away from Brisbane's City Centre making it easy for transportation. In addition, free parking is available for all.

Transport by bus

With a close proximity to Herston Bus Stop, many buses such as the 66, 330, 333 and 340 frequently pass through allowing for transport to the North and SOuth of Brisbane.
